HISTORY

Syafiq Ahmad, a native of Kedah, was the one to establish the Mat Motor company in 2019, and he was the founder of the company. This company solely sells motorcycles and accessories for motorcycles, all of which are exclusive to Mat Motor outlets and can only be purchased there. Since the beginning of 2019, Syafiq Ahmad has served as the Chief Executive Officer of Mat Motor.
